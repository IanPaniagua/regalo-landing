# Marketing Brief

## Brand & Product
- **Brand Name:** Regalos App
- **Product / Service:** Social ecosystem and app for automated birthday reminders, enriched social wishlists, and seamless group gift coordination (cost-splitting).
- **Tagline (optional):** Never forget a birthday — or stress about group gifts — again.

---

## Target Audience
<!-- Describe who you are trying to reach. Be as specific as possible. -->
- **Primary audience:** "Thoughtful Gifters" (aged 25–40), friend group organizers, and productivity/app enthusiasts.
- **Pain points:** Forgetting birthdays, not knowing what to gift, ruining the surprise by asking, the chaos of organizing group payments, and chasing friends for money via Venmo/Bizum.
- **Aspirations:** To effortlessly give the perfect gift, celebrate friends authentically, and avoid logistical stress/awkwardness.
- **Where they hang out online:** Reddit (r/gifts, r/SideProject, r/Frugal), TikTok, Instagram, Facebook Groups, WhatsApp, Product Hunt.

---

## Landing Page Goal
<!-- What is the ONE main action you want visitors to take on the landing page? -->
- **Primary CTA:** Join Beta Testing / Download Now (Sign up for waitlist).
- **Secondary goal (optional):** Validate the core problem, measure platform interest (iOS vs Android), and capture early adopters for qualitative feedback.

---

## Campaign Objectives
<!-- What do you want to achieve with this 5-day content cycle? -->
- [x] Increase brand awareness
- [x] Drive traffic to the landing page
- [x] Generate leads / sign-ups
- [ ] Boost sales / conversions
- [x] Build community / engagement
- [ ] Other: Validate the market need with real beta testers

---

## Brand Voice & Tone
<!-- How should the content feel? Check all that apply or describe freely. -->
- **Tone:** Authentic, relatable, casual, helpful, and transparent (building in public).
- **What to AVOID:** Corporate jargon, aggressive sales language (e.g., "Buy now!"), and overly polished/fake content.
- **Example brands with a voice you admire:** Notion, BeReal, Indie hackers sharing their real journeys.

---

## Key Message
<!-- The single most important thing you want your audience to understand or feel after seeing your content. -->
Remembering a birthday is easy, but getting the gift right and getting 10 friends to agree to pay for it is hard. Regalos App reduces months of stress into three taps: see whose turn it is, check their real profile for exactly what they want, and create a virtual pool to split the cost instantly.

---

## Unique Value Proposition
<!-- What makes your product/service different from competitors? -->
Unlike standard calendars or basic payment apps, Regalos App combines dynamic social profiles (real hobbies and exact sizes) with smart group gift coordination (automatic cost-splitting and dedicated chats). It builds an autonomous network where birthdays and preferences sync automatically as friends join.

---

## Proof Points (optional)
<!-- Any stats, testimonials, case studies, or achievements that build credibility -->
- Built to solve a widespread personal problem: tired of forgetting birthdays and Venmo chaos.
- Product currently in high-demand validation (Beta testing phase).
- Designed with insights from real "friend group organizers."

---

## Content Constraints (optional)
<!-- Any rules or limits the content creator should follow -->
- **Topics to avoid:** Hard selling without engaging first; purely promotional posts on Reddit (must engage authentically in threads first).
- **Must-include elements:** Genuine stories regarding gift-giving pains, link/guide toward the beta opt-in, use of native formats.
- **Visual style:** Raw and authentic over highly polished. Short videos filmed on phone (POV style) for TikTok/Reels, and screenshots of build-in-public metrics for Twitter/LinkedIn.

---

## Additional Context (optional)
<!-- Anything else that will help generate better content. Season, launch date, promotions, etc. -->
- **Current Phase:** Product Validation Phase (8-Week Sprint with the goal of 500+ visits and 50+ beta signups).
- **Core Marketing Strategy:** Zero budget, high-effort organic approach with heavy use of n8n automation for content scheduling and beta user follow-ups.
- **Target Submarkets:** The landing page and initial outreach target English, Spanish (native market/LatAm), and German speakers.
