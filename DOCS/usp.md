# Unique Selling Proposition (USP) & Value Proposition - Regalos App

## 1. The Core USP (The Power Statement)
> **"Regalos App is the only social ecosystem that not only reminds you of birthdays but connects you with your friends' real tastes and allows you to organize and pay for group gifts seamlessly and stress-free."**

The current market problem isn't knowing *when* the birthday is (Facebook or the Apple calendar already do that). The real problem is knowing **what to gift** and **how to organize the payment among several people** without chasing down defaulters or using Excel spreadsheets.

That is the gap that Regalos App dominates.

---

## 2. The Three Core Differentiators (Core Features)

### A. Enriched Social Profiles (The end of "I don't know what to get them")
- **The Pain:** Guessing what to gift, gifting something they don't like, or having to ask directly and ruining the surprise.
- **The Solution (Regalos App):** A public/private profile where each user lists their **real hobbies, their sizes, and their exact gift preferences**. By connecting with them (@username or web invitation), you automatically discover the perfect gift based on real data.

### B. Smart Group Gift Coordination *(Killer Feature)*
- **The Pain:** Creating a WhatsApp group, pitching ideas, debating budgets, having someone put their credit card down, and then having to chase 5 people via Venmo/PayPal/Bizum so they pay their share.
- **The Solution (Regalos App):** Instant group creation with an automatic cost-splitting calculator. Visual tracking of "Who has paid" and "Who is pending" linked to an internal chat focused solely and exclusively on coordinating that person's surprise.

### C. Autonomous Network and Smart Synchronization
- **The Pain:** Sitting down on January 1st to manually enter your friends' birthdays into a digital calendar with alarms.
- **The Solution (Regalos App):** The network builds itself. As you connect with friends (or your contacts install the app), birthdays and reminders sync and update on their own. If someone adds a new hobby to their profile, the network knows.
